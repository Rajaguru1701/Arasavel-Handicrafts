Who We Are

Engineered Precision,
Steeped in Sacred Tradition

Led by Aravinth, a professional Mechanical Engineer turned master festival craftsman, our workshop unites modern technical layout planning with centuries-old heritage methods. Based in Madurai, we specialize in weaving the massive visual identity of Tamil Nadu's grand temple festival cars (Ther Alankaram).

Our work is recognizable by its heavy velvet bases, vibrant traditional imagery featuring sacred peacocks, elephants, and temple iconography, all hand-outlined using traditional white thread tailoring techniques.

From the grand streets of Tamil Nadu to overseas Tamil diaspora temples across the US, Singapore, Malaysia, and Thailand, our regalia stands as a testament to unchanging devotion and flawless execution.
